Refunds & After-Sales

We want you to love every purchase. Here is how our refund and after-sales process works.

Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you are not satisfied with your order, you can request a full refund within 30 days of delivery.

To be eligible, items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

After-Sales Support

Our dedicated after-sales team is here to help with:

  • Exchanges for wrong size or color
  • Repairs for defective items
  • Replacements for damaged goods
  • General product care advice
